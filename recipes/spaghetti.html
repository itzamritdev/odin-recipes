Spaghetti

Description

Enjoy this classic spaghetti dish with a side of garlic bread and a fresh green salad for a complete meal. This hearty and flavorful recipe is perfect for a family dinner or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Combine Ingredients
  4. Season the Sauce
  5. Finish and Serve
