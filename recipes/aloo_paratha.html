Aloo Paratha

Description

Enjoy these delicious aloo parathas as a hearty breakfast or a satisfying lunch. The spiced potato filling combined with the crispy, golden-brown exterior makes for a delightful and comforting meal.

Ingredients

For the Dough:
  • Whole Wheat Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil or Ghee
For the Filling:
  • Potatoes
  • Green Chilies
  • Ginger
  • Garam Masala
  • Amchur
  • Salt
  • Cumin Seeds

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Make the Filling
  3. Shape the Parathas
  4. Cook the Parathas
  5. Serve
