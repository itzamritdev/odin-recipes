Ramen

Description

This classic homemade ramen is a perfect blend of hearty broth, tender noodles, and an array of fresh toppings, making it a versatile and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

For the Broth:
  • Chicken Broth
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green Onions
  • Miso Paste
For the Ramen:
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Chiecken Breast or Thighs
  • Soft-Boiled Eggs
  • Nori Sheets
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Chili Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Broth
  2. Cook the Ramen Noodle
  3. Cook the Soft-Boiled Eggs
  4. Prepare the Toppings
  5. Assemble the Ramen Bowls
  6. Serve
